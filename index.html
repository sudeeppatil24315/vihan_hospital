About Dr. Sujith Patil B.

Dr. Sujith Patil B. is a highly experienced Consultant Arthroscopic & Joint Replacement Surgeon dedicated to restoring mobility and improving the quality of life for his patients. With prestigious qualifications including MS Ortho and DNB Orthopaedics from New Delhi, he brings world-class expertise to Raichur.


Holding fellowships in Arthroscopy and Sports Medicine (FASM) as well as Joint Replacement (FIJR), Dr. Patil specializes in minimally invasive procedures, ensuring faster recovery times, less pain, and excellent long-term outcomes for trauma and joint replacement patients.
